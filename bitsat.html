Section 4 · Private Engineering

BITSAT — BITS Pilani

India's most prestigious private engineering university. BITS Pilani, Goa, and Hyderabad admit exclusively through BITSAT — not JEE scores.

Conducted byBITS Pilani
Max Score390 marks
CampusesPilani / Goa / Hyderabad
ModeComputer-Based Test
⚠ 2025 cutoffs from official BITS admissions portal (bitsadmission.com). BITS does NOT use JEE scores.

4.1 Eligibility

CriteriaDetails
StreamPCM — Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics all mandatory
Class 12 MarksMinimum 75% in PCM + English aggregate (60% for NRI/OCI)
YearPassed in 2024 or 2025, or appearing in 2026
ReservationNo category-based reservation — purely merit-based on BITSAT score
JEE ScoreNot considered at all. BITSAT score is the only criterion.

4.2 Exam Pattern

SectionQuestionsMarks EachTotal
