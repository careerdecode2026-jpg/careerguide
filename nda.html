Section 10 · Defence Forces

NDA — National Defence Academy

One of India's most prestigious career paths. Become a commissioned officer in the Army, Navy, or Air Force. PCM is mandatory for Navy and Air Force wings.

Conducted byUPSC
FrequencyTwice a year (April & September)
CostFree + Stipend during training
Age16.5 – 19.5 years

10.1 Eligibility

CriteriaDetails
GenderMale only (check latest UPSC notification for female eligibility updates following Supreme Court order)
Age16.5 to 19.5 years at time of course commencement
For Army WingAny stream — even without PCM
For Navy / Air Force WingPCM in Class 12 — mandatory
Physical FitnessGood physical fitness, eyesight standards, no history of major illness. Medical exam is thorough.
Marital StatusMust be unmarried

10.2 Exam Pattern

PaperMarksDurationSubjects
Paper I: Mathematics3002.5 hrsAlgebra, Matrices, Trigonometry, Calculus, Statistics, Probability, Vectors, 3D Geometry
Paper II: General Ability6002.5 hrsEnglish (200) + GK: Physics, Chemistry, Geography, History, Current Affairs (400)
SSB Interview9005 daysPsychology tests, Group tasks, Personal Interview. Assesses leadership and personality.
Total1800—Written + SSB. Final merit based on both combined.

10.3 Training & Pay After Selection

StageDetails
NDA Training3 years at NDA, Khadakwasla, Pune (joint training for all forces)
Service Academy1 year at IMA (Army) / INA (Navy) / AFA (Air Force)
Starting RankLieutenant / Sub-Lieutenant / Flying Officer on commissioning
Starting Pay~₹56,100–1,77,500/month + MSP ₹15,500 + HRA + Free accommodation + Ration
Career GrowthCaptain → Major → Lt Colonel → Colonel → Brigadier → Major General → Lt General → General
Additional BenefitsPension for life, free medical (family included), CSD canteen, free children's education, accommodation
Cost of NDA training: ₹0. The government fully funds training. Students receive a stipend during the 3-year course at NDA.
