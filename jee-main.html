1.1 What is JEE Main — Two Sessions Explained

JEE Main is conducted twice a year — Session 1 in January and Session 2 in April. You can appear in one or both. Your best score across both sessions is used for ranking. There is no disadvantage in appearing in both — in fact, most students attempt both to maximize their percentile.

Key Rule: Best of Session 1 and Session 2 is considered. Appearing in both sessions costs only a small additional registration fee and significantly improves your chances.
