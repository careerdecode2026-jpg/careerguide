3.1 What is JoSAA?

JoSAA (Joint Seat Allocation Authority) runs the centralized counselling for all government-funded premium engineering institutes. After both JEE Main and JEE Advanced results are declared, candidates register on josaa.nic.in, fill their college/branch preferences, and seats are allocated based on rank and preferences over 6 rounds.

Critical: Missing JoSAA deadlines means losing your seat permanently. Track all reporting dates very carefully once counselling begins.

3.3 Home State vs All India Quota

For NITs, 50% seats are Home State Quota (HS) — reserved for students who passed Class 12 from that state. The remaining 50% are All India Quota (AI). HS quota typically has significantly higher closing ranks (easier to get) compared to AI quota.

Example: MNIT Jaipur CSE — AI quota closing rank ~13,000. A Rajasthan student applying through HS quota may get in at a rank up to ~20,000–25,000.
