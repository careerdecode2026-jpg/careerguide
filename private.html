Section 5 · Private Colleges

Private College Entrance Exams

Good backup options after JEE. VIT, SRM, Manipal and state-level exams offer quality education at lower competition than JEE.

5.1 VITEEE — VIT University

DetailInformation
CampusesVellore (main), Chennai, Amaravati, Bhopal
EligibilityPCM with minimum 60% (45% for reserved). No age limit.
Pattern125 questions: Physics(35) + Chemistry(35) + Maths(40) + English(5) + Aptitude(10). Online CBT.
Marking+1 correct, NO negative marking. Duration: 2.5 hours.
Cutoff (Gen)CSE Vellore: Typically 90–100+ marks out of 125 for top branches
Fees (4yr incl. hostel)₹12–15 Lakh total
PlacementsAvg: ₹7–12 LPA. Highest: ₹44+ LPA. Top recruiters: TCS, Infosys, Amazon, Microsoft. ~85% placement.

5.2 SRMJEEE — SRM University

DetailInformation
CampusesKattankulathur (main), Delhi-NCR, Tiruchirappalli, Vadapalani
EligibilityPCM with 60% minimum. No age limit.
Fees (4yr)₹12–17 Lakh total
PlacementsAvg: ₹6–10 LPA. Recruiters: Amazon, Infosys, TCS, Wipro, Cognizant. ~80% placement.
