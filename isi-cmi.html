Section 9 · Mathematics & Statistics

ISI & CMI

India's most prestigious institutions for Mathematics, Statistics, and Computer Science. For exceptionally talented mathematics students with olympiad-level thinking.

Difficulty: These exams require olympiad-level mathematical thinking — significantly harder than JEE Mathematics. Most questions cannot be solved with standard Class 12 syllabus alone.

9.1 Indian Statistical Institute (ISI)

Established 1931, Kolkata. Declared Institute of National Importance by Government of India. One of the world's premier institutions for Statistics and Mathematics.

DetailInformation
Programs (UG)B.Stat (Hons) — Kolkata  |  B.Math (Hons) — Bangalore
EligibilityClass 12 with Mathematics. No minimum percentage specified but performance must be exceptional.
Entrance ExamISI Admission Test: UGA (B.Stat) and UGB (B.Math). Highly conceptual Math + Statistics + Subjective answers.
Fees (3yr)~₹10,000–15,000/year tuition. Hostel ~₹2,000/month. Total: Very affordable (~₹1–2 Lakh).
Top CareersPhD at Princeton/MIT/Cambridge, Actuarial Science, Quant Finance (Jane Street, DE Shaw), Data Science at top firms, IAS, Academia.

9.2 Chennai Mathematical Institute (CMI)

Private deemed university. World-class for Mathematics and Theoretical Computer Science.

DetailInformation
Programs (UG)B.Sc. (Hons) Math & CS  |  B.Sc. (Hons) Math & Physics  |  B.Sc. (Hons) Math
Entrance ExamCMI Entrance Test: Mathematics (compulsory) + CS or Physics (optional). Olympiad-level difficulty.
Fees (3yr)~₹2 Lakh/year tuition. Total ~₹6 Lakh. Scholarships available for meritorious students.
Top CareersPhD at IIT/IISc/international universities, Theoretical CS research, Quant roles, Software at Google/Amazon.
Appearing for Math Olympiad (RMO → INMO → IMO) during Class 9–12 is the best preparation path for ISI and CMI, and also significantly strengthens JEE Advanced preparation.
