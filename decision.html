12.3 Rank vs College vs Branch — Priority Guide

This is the most common dilemma. Here is the recommended priority order for most students:
PriorityRule
1Old IIT (Bombay/Delhi/Madras) in ANY branch > NIT Trichy/Warangal CSE
2For Top 5 IITs: Institute brand > Branch choice
3For Tier 2 IITs: CSE at good NIT may be better than Civil/Chemical at newer IIT
4BITS Pilani CSE > Most NITs for CSE (better flexibility, dual degree)
5State government college > Expensive low-ranked private college
6NDA > Any engineering option if you have the passion and physical fitness
Common Mistake: Do not choose an expensive private college ranked 200+ over a government state college with decent placements. The state college will have lower fees and often comparable or better industry connections in your home region.
