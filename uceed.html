Section 7 · Design

UCEED — B.Des at IITs

For students with creative and analytical minds. Bachelor of Design at IITs — a completely different path from engineering, testing design aptitude and visual thinking.

Conducted byIIT Bombay
ProgramB.Des (Bachelor of Design)
Total Seats~225 across all IITs
ExamJanuary each year

7.1 Eligibility

CriteriaDetails
StreamANY stream (Science, Commerce, Arts all eligible for most IITs). PCM students fully eligible.
ExceptionsIIT Guwahati & IIT Roorkee: Science stream (PCM) only. IIITDM Jabalpur: PCM or PCB only.
Class 12Must have passed all 5 subjects. No minimum percentage required.
Age (Gen/EWS/OBC)Born on or after October 1, 2000 (for 2025 cycle)
AttemptsMaximum 2 attempts (Class 12 year + 1 year after)

7.2 Exam Pattern

PartTypeTests
Part AComputer-based (MCQ + MSQ + NAT)Visualization, Environmental Science, Design Aptitude, Observation, Analytical Reasoning. Score determines qualification.
Part BPaper-based sketching / drawingEvaluated only if Part A cleared. Tests artistic skill, creativity, 3D visualization.
Final rank = combined Part A + Part B score. No textbook recommended for UCEED — it tests inherent aptitude and developed visual skills.
