6.1 What is IISER?

IISERs (Indian Institutes of Science Education and Research) are government-funded premier science universities. They offer a 5-year BS-MS Dual Degree in pure sciences — Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, Data Science, and interdisciplinary programs. From 2024 onwards, admission is exclusively through the IISER Aptitude Test (IAT).

Important: IISER is NOT suitable for students who want direct engineering jobs after 4 years. It is ideal for students who love science and are open to PhD, research labs, or academic careers.

6.5 Fees — Highly Subsidized

IISER is one of the most affordable premier science programs in India. Annual tuition: approximately ₹15,000–30,000. Hostel + Mess: approximately ₹40,000–60,000 per year. Total 5-year cost: approximately ₹3–5 Lakh.

DST-INSPIRE Scholarship: ₹80,000/year + ₹20,000 research grant available for top-ranked students pursuing pure sciences. Applies to IISER students who scored in top 1% of their board.
