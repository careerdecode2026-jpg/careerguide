Section 8 · Architecture

Architecture — NATA & JEE Paper 2

5-year B.Arch program for students with interest in design, drawing, spatial thinking, and creativity combined with analytical skills. Mathematics in Class 12 is mandatory.

8.1 Two Routes to B.Arch

RouteExamColleges
Route 1JEE Main Paper 2 (B.Arch)NITs, IIT Roorkee, SPA Delhi, GFTIs — via JoSAA counselling
Route 2NATA (National Aptitude Test in Architecture)Private and state government architecture colleges across India

8.2 Eligibility

CriteriaDetails
Mandatory SubjectMathematics in Class 12 — compulsory for all B.Arch programs
Minimum Marks50% aggregate in Class 12 (for NATA). 75% in PCM for JEE Paper 2 route.
Drawing AptitudeStrong drawing / sketching ability is essential — tested in exam
