Section 11 · Marine Engineering

Merchant Navy — IMU CET

High-paying career at sea. Marine Engineering officers are in demand globally — ships carry 90% of world trade. PCM is mandatory.

EntranceIMU CET (Indian Maritime University)
Program4-yr B.Tech Marine Engineering
Starting Pay₹50,000–1.5L/month

11.1 Overview

DetailInformation
EligibilityPCM mandatory. Minimum 60% in PCM. Age: 17–25 years. Good eyesight and physical fitness required.
Program4-year B.Tech Marine Engineering OR 3-year BSc Nautical Science
Fees (4yr)₹8–15 Lakh total at IMU campuses. Private maritime institutes: ₹20–30 Lakh.
Key CollegesIMU Chennai, IMU Mumbai, IMU Kolkata, IMU Visakhapatnam, T.S. Chanakya (Mumbai), MERI Kolkata
Career ProgressionJunior Engineer → Second Engineer → Chief Engineer
Salary (Chief Engineer)₹3–8 Lakh/month — among the highest-paid non-IT careers in India
Starting Salary₹50,000–1.5 Lakh/month after training
Lifestyle Consideration6–9 months at sea, 3 months at home. Long periods away from family. Physically demanding.
Merchant Navy is one of the few careers where you can become financially independent in your 20s without an IIT degree. The trade-off is time away from home during sea postings.
